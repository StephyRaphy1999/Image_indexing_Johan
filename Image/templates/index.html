About Us

Our aim is to create a platform that helps people interested in photography to showcase their talents and also helps them to make a living. The platform will help to create a repository of user-uploaded images. Contests are introduced in order to find the hidden talent in people around us and encourage users to add to the repository of images. The platform catalogues images according to various attributes and can be searched for by users. The collection of images can be purchased or downloaded by users for free or for a price for premium images.
